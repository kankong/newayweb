WHO WE ARE

The NEWAY Industry Vision

The NEWAY Industries team works together to provide a value added service to the typical material procurement process. From manufacturing to installation we work to control the quality, scheduling, storage, delivery, insurance and warranty of our trusted industry partner's products. All sales are processed by our team and managed inside of a unified framework with built in quality control that is constantly evolving.

The founders of NEWAY Industries have a long history in the trades and construction industry as well as systems building and contract development for insurance and risk management. As our team advanced the problem solving skills necessary to succeed we developed specific practices to avoid the hazards and delays that plague the construction process. These often repetitive and unnecessary delays run up costs unexpectedly and hurt your bottom line so we created innovative solutions to streamline the process.
